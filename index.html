Process

How We Work

Our process flow follows document reference BMB/8.1/F02/00 to connect customer requirements with planning, production, QA, and dispatch in one controlled system.

01.

Customer Requirement & Marketing

Management and customer-related process captures requirements, scope, and quality expectations before execution.

02.

Purchasing & Planning

Material planning, procurement, and production planning are finalized to ensure controlled workflow and delivery readiness.

03.

Manufacturing, Inspection & Storage

Production is carried out with in-process QA inspection, preservation, and storage controls to maintain product quality.

04.

Packaging, Dispatch & Continual Improvement

Finished parts are packaged and dispatched, while QMS performance, maintenance, risk, training, and improvements are reviewed continuously.
